Goldsky supports the following pipeline sources:

## [Subgraphs](/mirror/sources/subgraphs)

Mirror data from our community subgraphs or from your own subgraphs into any sink.

## [Direct Indexing](/mirror/sources/direct-indexing)

Mirror entire blockchains into your database for analysis, or filter/transform them to what you need.

## [NFT Data (tech preview)](/mirror/sources/nft-data)

Use our highly reactive NFT datasources and push them, filtering for what you need, into your database.
